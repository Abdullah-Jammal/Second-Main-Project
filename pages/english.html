Ta3leem platform features

Diversity of educational materials

The education platform offers a variety of educational materials for all educational levels, for several teachers, and in different ways

Easy access and presence online

The Taalim platform enjoys ease of registration and access to educational materials via the Internet Learners can access the content at any time and from anywhere via a computer application or mobile device.

Interactive and sharing

The education platform allows interaction between learners and content through discussions and participation files, group discussions, and more. It also encourages collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.

Providing rich, multimedia content

Courses available through the Taalim platform include content rich in various media, such as Videos, articles, interactive tests, and guided lectures, in addition to mind maps and more.

Flexibility in schedule

Allows learners to learn according to their own schedules. They can submit and complete assignments decisions according to their own priorities

Evaluate and track progress

Taaleem offers an assessment system that allows learners to track their progress and understand where they need to go Optimization. It can also provide reports and statistics for learners and teachers.
