Features

Variety of educational materials

Ta3leem platform offers a variety of educational materials for all academic levels and for several teachers in different way.

Ease of access and online presence

The ta3leem platform has the ease of registration and access to educational materials via the internet. where learners can access the content at any time and from anywhere through the computer or mobile application.

Interactive sharing

The education platform allows interaction between learners and content through discussions, file sharing, group posts, and more. It also encourages collaborative learning and Knowledge sharing.

Provide rich and multimedia content

The courses available through the education platform include content rich in various media. Such as videos, articles, interactive tests, and guidance lectures, in addition to mental maps and others.

Flexibility in schedule

Learners are allowed to learn according to their own schedule. They can submit assignments and complete their home work according to their own priorities.

Evaluate and track progress

Ta3leem platform offers an assessment system that allows learners to track their progress and understand where they need to improve. It can also provide reports and statistics for learners and teachers.
