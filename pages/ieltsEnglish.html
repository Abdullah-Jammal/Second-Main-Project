Registration instructions

1

Take the exam through the link

https://myleveltest.com/

2

Create an account on the site

You must enter 1- Name 2- Address 3- Email 4- Country

3

Proof of ownership of the email address

After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email detailing your course schedule, timings and any other necessary information

Pay the test fee

Once you pay the fee, you will be immediately directed to the test pages.

We strongly recommend that you plan a suitable time and place to take the level test before completing the payment process, as you may not be able to access the test if you do not do so immediately. It is recommended to choose a quiet place and ensure a stable internet connection

Once you complete the test, you will immediately receive via email a test completion certificate and a detailed report on your current level with recommended actions to improve your level.

Placement test fees: Students pay the placement test fees amounting to 23,500 thousand dinars at the education company or via credit card.

Register for the course

Once the course is available, you can contact the Educational Consultant at Talim with your test report to register for any of the recommended courses by contacting (Mohamed Karim Hussein)

07732525761

Mohammedkareem@ta3leem.iq

After the registration process is successfully completed, you will receive a confirmation email containing course details, including exact timings and any other necessary information. In addition, the following must be attached to the letter:

1- Benefits of the placement test, as it helps determine the current language level of the participants.

2- A list of announced courses, where the types of available courses are explained.

3- The education system used to study the English language, which explains how to organize the educational content and resources used in the course.

4- The benefits of using the English language teaching platform, where the focus is on the advantages of using the platform in the learning process.
