Registration instructions

1

Visit the Test Your Level website

https://myleveltest.com

2

Create an account for the placement test

To complete the account creation step, you will be asked to verify ownership of your email address.

3

Pay for a level test

Once you log in, you will be directed to the payment page to pay for the test online using a card

4

Take a level test

Once you process the payment, you will be directed immediately to the test pages. If you do not perform a level test immediately after payment, you may not be able to reach the test.

5

Receive the test completion certificate and report

Once you complete the test, you will immediately receive via email a test completion certificate and a detailed report on your current level with recommended actions to improve your levels.

6

Course recommendations

Based on the test results, courses from UniHouse that best match your current proficiency level and learning needs are automatically recommended.

7

Enroll in the recommended course

Contact the educational advisor at our institution with your test report to register for any of the recommended courses by contacting the assistant teacher (Amjad Bashar Obaid) on phone 07727301834 or email com.gmail@upm.bashar.a

8

Confirm and start

After successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email detailing your course schedule, timings and any other necessary information
